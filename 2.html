What is TalentGro Global?

TalentGro Global is a career and education platform that helps students and professionals go from learning to earning. Think AI-powered career counseling, skill courses, live internships, mock interviews, resume tools, and expert mentorship — all in one place. Two Types of Rewards

Every verified volunteer action earns you coins. The more impactful the action, the more valuable the reward.

🪙
Standard Coin

DiSha Coin (DSC)

100 DSC = ₹50 off

Earned for every verified volunteer activity — writing articles, attending events, helping your community, and more. Use them at checkout on TalentGro to get real money off courses, career tools, and assessments.

  • ✓ Minimum 200 DSC to redeem per transaction
  • ✓ Valid for 18 months from last activity
  • ✓ Non-transferable — yours to keep and grow
💎
Premium Token

DiSha Premium (DSC-P)

1 DSC-P = 5× value of DSC

Earned only for high-impact contributions — leading events, completing mentorship cycles, major open-source contributions, or representing Disha at a national conference. DSC-P unlocks exclusive career services that standard coins alone cannot access.

  • 💎 Unlocks: AI career counseling, mentorship, placement assistance
  • 💎 Stacks with DSC for an even bigger discount
  • 💎 Marks you as a top-tier contributor

Fair Play Policy

To keep rewards meaningful for everyone, each category has a monthly earning limit, and the same activity has a 48-hour cooldown before it can be submitted again.

✅

Verification Timeline

Auto-verified activities (QR, GitHub, referral codes) credit within 2 hours. Activities requiring review are processed within 72 hours.

🔒

Quality Standards

All content submissions go through a quality check. High-quality work gets credited; low-quality or duplicate content is returned for revision.
