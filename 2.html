Section 1.1

Core Currency Definition

A single, unified token bridges both platforms — earned through impact on Disha, spent on growth at TalentGro Global.

About TalentGro Global

TalentGro Global is an education and career development platform that helps students and professionals build skills, gain practical experience, and improve their career opportunities. The company provides AI-based career counseling, career assessments, skill development courses, internships with live projects, mentorship from industry experts, placement assistance, resume-building tools, and job portal access. It also works with colleges, universities, and organizations by offering faculty development programs, AI upskilling, placement training, and career mapping solutions. Its main goal is to bridge the gap between learning and employment by helping individuals become job-ready through real-world training and career guidance.

🛡️ Anti-Gaming Cap

Hard ceiling of 2,000 DSC earned per calendar month from any single category. Users cannot earn more than 5,000 DSC total per month regardless of activity count.

⏱️ Cooldown Rules

Same activity type has a 48-hour cooldown window. Bulk submissions of the same task within 24 hours triggers manual review before any coins are credited.

✅ Verification Queue

Auto-verified activities (GitHub, QR, referral) credit within 2 hours. Editor/Admin-reviewed activities have a 72-hour SLA. Disputed submissions go to a 2-reviewer panel.

🔒 Quality Gate

Content with plagiarism score >20% (Copyscape/Turnitin API) is auto-rejected. Community reports of low-quality work trigger a 7-day earning suspension pending review.

Business Sustainability Note: At the base rate of ₹50 per 100 DSC, and a 5,000 DSC monthly earning cap, the maximum monthly liability per user is ₹2,500 in discounts. Since each volunteer also drives ₹X in platform value (content, events, community growth), the ROI is strongly positive. The 30% maximum discount cap ensures TalentGro retains at minimum 70% revenue on every transaction. DSC-P prestige tokens keep high-value services gated, creating aspirational tiers that drive long-term retention without eroding the P&L.
