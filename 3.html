Section 3.4

Viral Growth Guardrails

Anti-abuse policies ensure all referrals drive genuine value for the education and wellness ecosystem.

🚫 Anti-Sybil Validation

Accounts registering from the same IP address or device fingerprint within a 30-day window are flagged. Self-referrals will lead to account suspension.

🔒 Verified Action Gate

Points are only awarded when the referred friend completes a verified task earning at least 50 DSC. Empty account registrations receive zero rewards.

Cap on Viral Rewards

Maximum of 30 successful referral payouts (4,500 DSC) can be earned per user per month. Milestone bonuses do not count towards this monthly cap.

⚖️ Manual Review Queue

Reaching the "Viral Ambassador" milestone (25 invites) triggers an automated quality review of all referred activities prior to releasing the 5 DSC-P and physical award.

Sustainability Note: Introducing referred members through a validated action gate keeps acquisition costs highly positive. At an acquisition rate of 300 DSC total liability (150 to referrer, 150 to referee), the platform spends ₹150 in course discount credits. In return, the platform receives two active contributors completing verified content, event organization, or code. This viral loops engine reduces external marketing expenses to zero.
