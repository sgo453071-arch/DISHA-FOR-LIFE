Ecosystem Documentation

Core Platform Modules

Understanding the heart of the Disha × TalentGro ecosystem. Here is how our four core modules work together to empower volunteers and build a stronger community.

🪙

Token Economy (Tokenomy)

Our gamified Token Economy (Tokenomy) acts as the financial engine of the ecosystem. Volunteers earn DiSha Coins (DSC) and prestige DiSha Premium (DSC-P) tokens for performing verified social actions on Disha. These tokens are redeemable directly at checkout on TalentGro Global, giving volunteers access to skill development courses, AI-based career counseling, career assessments, internships, and expert mentorship. This creates a sustainable loop where students are incentivized to contribute their time and talent to help society, receiving valuable career training and certifications in return.

Explore Tokenomics →
📣

Refer & Earn

The Refer & Earn system is designed to drive viral community growth by encouraging volunteers to invite their friends and peers. Both the referrer and the invited friend receive a starting reward of +150 DSC as soon as the friend completes their first verified volunteer activity. To motivate students to grow their networks, we offer targeted milestones—such as the "Community Builder" challenge, which awards an extra +2,000 DSC and auto-promotes the user to the Catalyst tier for inviting 10 members. This milestone-driven loop ensures the platform grows organically with zero marketing spend, while introducing verified, active contributors to the ecosystem.

View Referral Hub →
🏆

Proof of Impact

The Proof of Impact page acts as a public Wall of Fame, serving as a powerful resume builder and recruiter attraction tool for students. It displays verified cards for top volunteers, showcasing their lifetime contributions, such as Shourya Garg's record of 120 hours logged and 8,400 DSC coins earned. Recruiters or universities can verify these stats on the spot by clicking the interactive "Verify Credentials" buttons, which generate high-fidelity digital certificates. This instant credibility, combined with dynamic category filters, turns volunteer work into tangible career assets, making the platform highly attractive to students looking to prove their skills.

See Wall of Fame →
🚀

Active Programs

Our Active Programs portal serves as the launching pad for social action and real-world leadership development. It outlines current open campaigns, such as the flagship Disha Campus Ambassador Program which establishes student-run chapters (consisting of 1 Chapter Lead, 5 Core Members, and 20 Volunteers per college) to coordinate on-campus wellness drives and green initiatives. Students can browse open roles, understand the clear volunteer breakdowns, see estimated coin payouts, and apply instantly through an interactive simulation interface. This connects interested students directly to social problems they care about while rewarding them with simulated sign-up credits (+100 DSC) to start their volunteer journey.
