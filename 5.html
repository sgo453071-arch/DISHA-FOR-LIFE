Disha Campus Ambassador Program

Chapter Network

Establish a Disha Chapter in your university, organize events, recruit volunteers, and act as a bridge between the campus community and Disha Trust. Drive change on the ground while developing strong leadership and communication skills.

Chapter Structure (Per College)

1 Chapter Lead Coordinates events & HQ comms
5 Core Members Manage media, tech & logistics
20 Volunteers Execute ground awareness drives
🎁 PROGRAM BENEFITS & DSC REWARDS
  • Chapter Lead: 1,200 DSC + 3 DSC-P + Direct Industry Mentorship
  • Core Members: 800 DSC + 1 DSC-P + Co-branded Certificate
  • Volunteers: 400 DSC + Skill Badges on TalentGro

Emotional Wellness Campaign

Health & Wellness

Support mental health advocacy and wellness education. Facilitate regional online webinars, set up interactive on-campus mental health booths, and distribute peer-support resource kits developed by professional counselors.

Campaign Team Structure

3 Wellness Facilitators Host webinars and group sessions
10 Campaign Volunteers Moderate forums and share resources
🎁 PROGRAM BENEFITS & DSC REWARDS
  • Wellness Facilitators: 700 DSC + 1 DSC-P + Mental Health Advocate Badge
  • Campaign Volunteers: 350 DSC + Peer Outreach Certificate
